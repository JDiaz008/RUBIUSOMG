Rubén Doblas Gundersen, Known worldwide as El Rubius or RubiusOMG, he was born on February 13, 1990, in Mijas, Málaga, Spain. He is the son of a Norwegian mother and a Spanish father, who spent his childhood between Spain and Norway. He started his YouTube channel in 2011 and quickly became one of the most influential Spanish-language content creators, standing out for his humor, creativity, and youth-friendly style. His content includes gameplays, vlogs, sketches, and live streams, reaching millions of followers on platforms like YouTube and Twitch. El Rubius has achieved world record live viewership and has been recognized as one of the biggest Spanish-speaking YouTubers. He has also participated in projects outside of YouTube, such as the comic "Virtual Hero" (which was even adapted into an animated series) and various digital entertainment events. He currently resides in Andorra and continues to be a leader in digital culture and online entertainment, amassing millions of followers on his social media platforms.
